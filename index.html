Help & support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?
Click on any offerwall, complete surveys, sign-ups, or other offers. Points are credited automatically in real-time when you complete offers.
Why are my points on hold?
Points over 4000 are placed on hold for verification to prevent fraud. They will be approved by an administrator within 3-5 business days.
How do I redeem points?
Go to the Shop page, select a cashout option, and submit your request. You'll receive payment within 3-5 business days after approval.
What are the postback URLs?
Postback URLs allow offerwalls to send completion data directly to our system for automatic crediting. Contact support for integration details.
How do bonus codes work?
Bonus codes are special codes that give you free points when redeemed. They have expiration dates and usage limits. Check the Redeem Code page for available codes.
